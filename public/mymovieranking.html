The Best Movies Ever By Camelia Pana

My Favourite 3 movies of all-times

Megalopolis

Like wandering through the dreams of one of the most important filmmakers of all time.

Look Into My Eyes

“Look Into My Eyes,” a portrait of mediums and their clients, is a nonfiction movie where the whole point is to watch people, listen to what they say, and think about the emotions they feel as they tell their stories.

Anora

New York City can be an icy place even without its freezing temperatures. Colder, if you aren’t blessed with the basic privileges to survive in its margins.
